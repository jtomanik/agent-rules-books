# Comprehensive Behavior-Preserving Structural Audit

The behavior of a mature pricing component is protected by fast focused tests. Perform a comprehensive end-to-end structural audit without adding features or changing observable behavior.

The component contains long mixed-level functions, repeated calculations, feature envy, primitive clusters, broad mutation, nested conditionals, duplicated error paths, a god module, speculative abstractions, and a hierarchy that no longer represents meaningful variation.

Cover what counts as structural improvement, non-negotiable safety, smell diagnosis, preferred and catalog moves, functions, classes and modules, conditionals, data and mutation, errors, testing, forbidden patterns, code-generation choices, stopping, and final review. Recommend the next small transformations and stop before speculative redesign.
