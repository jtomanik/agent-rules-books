# Resolve a Vendor Relationship Source Dispute

The Product context uses `Assortment`, `MerchandisingGroup`, and `Availability`. Its only marketplace vendor controls a changing `CatalogTree`, `CategoryNode`, `ListingState`, and `MerchantOffer` taxonomy that merchandisers reject for internal planning.

Two reviewers disagree about the exact source guidance:

- One says a single unavoidable vendor and cheaper short-term adoption require a Conformist relationship.
- The other says a foreign taxonomy that would corrupt local language requires an Anticorruption Layer even when there is only one vendor.

They also dispute whether one synchronous consumer requires RPC, whether two asynchronous consumers justify messaging, and whether events should carry enough information or require query-back.

Resolve only this source-interpretation dispute. State the exact relationship-selection and integration-style conditions, where translation belongs, and which facts would change the decision. Do not perform a complete product-model audit.
