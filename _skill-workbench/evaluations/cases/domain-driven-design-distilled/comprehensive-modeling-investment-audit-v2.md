# Comprehensive Modeling Investment Audit

Perform a comprehensive end-to-end modeling audit for an order-to-cash product spanning Catalog, Pricing, Ordering, Inventory, Fulfillment, Billing, and Support.

The teams share overloaded terms, copy one domain package, let a vendor taxonomy shape local concepts, use large transaction-spanning object graphs, publish trivial events, and place most business behavior in application services. Some capabilities are strategically differentiating; others are generic and stable.

Cover where modeling investment is justified, subdomain classification, context boundaries and relationships, local language, integration styles, tactical patterns, Aggregate minimalism, application services, technology boundaries, collaboration, practicality, accelerated modeling, delivery, testing, and final review. Keep recommendations proportionate to business value and team constraints.
